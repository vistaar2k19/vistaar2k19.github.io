About IGIT Sarang

Indira Gandhi Institute of Technology is one of Odisha’s premiere govt. technical institutes. Flanked by nature’s greenery, it also stands exemplar of modern architecture. Spread over an area covering 179 acres, it integrates two academic buildings, hostels, staff quarters and a sprawling playground (Dr. M. P. Mishra Memorial Stadium). The institute boasts of eight departments, well equipped with highly qualified faculty and adequately equipped laboratories. With research activities well comparable to any other leading institutes around the world, more than 50 research papers are published in journals and conferences of National and International repute, every year. Within a short span of three decades, the institute has grown in reputation and academics at National levels. A great achievement being “The first Govt. Engg. College in the state to have accreditation from NBA(AICTE) because of academic excellence.

About Our Department

The B.Tech programme in Electronics and Telecommunication begun in the year 2008 with an approval from AICTE. Since then the department has strived towards its mission of crafting engineers with remarkable ingenuity, versatility and a heart for indigenous technology. The department laboratories are well equipped with state-of-the-art technology. The department also excels in the research field.

About Vistaar

Vistaar is a one-of-its-kind techno-cultural fest hosted by Department of Electronics and Telecommunication, Indira Gandhi Institute of Technology, Sarang. It aims to providing a platform to all electronics and telecommunication enthusiasts to come together and share their knowledge while enjoying the rich cultural heritage of IGIT, Sarang. It aims to bring all the ignited minds to a common platform and discuss latest developments in electronics industry which would be beneficial to the students’ academic development. It also aims at acquainting the future engineers with latest skills and technology and the methods of the industry.
